Andrés' Bananas

Andrés Jaime came to Norway from his home country Colombia in 2014. He stayed for 1 year before going back to Colombia to complete his Bachelor. Now he´s back in Norway, but this time with his amazing foodtruck with traditional Colombian Patacones. Patacones are twice-fried and sprinkled plantains (bananas) with salt for a crunchy, delicious bite that can be dipped in guacamole or sauce, or topped with shredded meat!


Come try his amazing Patacones at Gløshaugen and have a meal you won’t regret!
