Testing of the website

Administrative details

The name of the fictional client is Andres Bananas, and is the sole proprietor of Andres bananas foodtruck

Tester audience

Seeing that our intended audience is for students studying at gløshaugen who need a little food.
We went in the corridors and asked students if they had some time to spare to test out our webpage for a food ordering system.

Testing platforms

The testing platforms depended on situation, we had published the page on folk.ntnu and gave the link to students after asking if they would be willing to test out our page.
This resulted in different operative systems(macOS and Windows) on different screensizes. The browsers were primaraly safari and google chrome in these cases.
We also brought our own laptops and asked other students to test the website on those, where we had opened the page in opera and firefox.

We had brought our own laptops, so that the students wouldnt have to hassle with getting up their own.
The platforms we tested on where a windows 16.8 inch, and a macbook 13inch in different browsers like chrome, opera, firefox and safari
